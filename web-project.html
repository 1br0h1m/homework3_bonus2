Introduction

Welcome to the official page of Team 19's Website Project! This project is part of the SITE 1101 Principles of Information Systems course at ADA University. It is designed to showcase our skills in web development and collaboration.

Our Goals

Our primary goal is to create a functional and visually appealing website that reflects our understanding of web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, this project emphasizes teamwork, creativity, and technical problem-solving.

Key Features

  • Informative sections about team members and our coding activities.
  • Interactive and visually engaging content.
  • Innovative use of programming for creating digital art.

Learning Journey with Codecademy

The creators of this website utilized Codecademy as a platform to enhance their web development skills. Through hands-on practice and guided projects, the team developed a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
